The Rest of the House

Accessibility

There are five off-street parking spaces in front of the house, and the one closest to the mailbox is a wider, 10-foot spot. From the parking area, there is a partially accessible sidewalk (a little narrow at one point) toward the front door where there are two stairs up to the front porch with a handrail on the left. The front door is about 35-inches wide with a threshold bump, and then most internal doorways (some with threshold bumps) are about 28-inches wide. The main floor layout includes living room, dining room, full bathroom (semi-accessible), very small half bathroom (non-accessible), the kitchen, the Charcoal Room, and the Lavendar Room. All of the main floor common spaces are hardwood or linoleum, and both bedrooms are carpeted. There is a 90-degree turn staircase that goes up to the Green and Pink Rooms (both carpeted). There is a 90-degree turn staircase that goes down into the basement (rough concrete floor) where the washer and dryer units are. If you have any other accessibily questions, please don't hesitate to ask in your email to us.

The Right Fit

We're all pretty darn laid back and friendly, if we don't say so ourselves.
